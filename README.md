# geospatial-projects-
Geospatial analysis projects using SQL, QGIS, including spatial data visualization, mapping, and basic GIS analysis techniques.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Projects

### 1. Greenspaces and Parks Analysis – Cardiff
- Mapped and visualized the distribution of greenspaces and public parks across Cardiff
- Used spatial data to identify accessibility and coverage of green areas
- Applied symbology and classification techniques to highlight geographic patterns
- Insights can support urban planning and environmental management decisions

---

### 2. Crime Analysis and Spatial Relationships – Cardiff
- Analyzed spatial distribution of crime data across Cardiff
- Explored relationships between crime occurrences and geographic factors
- Created heatmaps and thematic maps to identify high-risk areas
- Demonstrated ability to derive insights from spatial datasets for public safety applications

---

### 3. Noise Exposure Analysis to Schools – Cardiff
- Assessed environmental noise exposure (road, rail, and industrial sources) affecting schools
- Integrated multiple spatial datasets to evaluate impact zones
- Performed proximity and overlay analysis to identify high-risk areas
- Produced visual outputs to support environmental and urban planning decisions

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Spatial Query & Advanced Analysis (SQL + QGIS)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
### 4. Environmental Pollution Hotspot Analysis
- Performed spatial queries using SQL within QGIS to identify pollution hotspots
- Analyzed environmental datasets to detect high-risk areas
- Used attribute filtering and spatial conditions to extract meaningful patterns
- Demonstrated integration of SQL with geospatial analysis workflows

---

### 5. Care Homes in Flood Risk Areas
- Identified care home locations within flood-prone zones using spatial queries
- Applied spatial joins and intersection analysis
- Highlighted vulnerable infrastructure for risk assessment and planning
- Produced maps to support decision-making in emergency and urban planning contexts

---

### 6. Urban Trees Near Major Roads (Environmental Stress Analysis)
- Queried and identified urban trees within a defined buffer distance (e.g., 50km) of major roads
- Applied buffer analysis and proximity-based spatial queries
- Assessed environmental stress exposure factors affecting urban vegetation
- Visualized results to highlight potential ecological impact zones
